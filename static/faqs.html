Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pestector, our plant disease classification system, and how to get the most out of our service.

General Questions

What is Pestector?

Pestector is an AI-powered plant disease classification system designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. By simply uploading a photo of the affected plant, our advanced machine learning model can diagnose the disease and provide treatment recommendations.

How accurate is Pestector?

Our classification model has been trained on the comprehensive PlantVillage dataset and achieves an accuracy of over 98% on test data. However, real-world conditions may vary, and factors like image quality, lighting, and disease progression can affect results. We display a confidence level with each prediction to help you gauge the reliability of the diagnosis.

Which plants and diseases can Pestector identify?

Pestector can identify diseases in 14 different plant types, including apple, tomato, potato, corn, grape, and more. Our system recognizes 38 different classes, including healthy plants and various diseases like leaf blight, bacterial spot, powdery mildew, and many others. You can view the complete list in our Disease Library.

Scanning & Analysis

How do I take the best photo for analysis?

For the most accurate results, follow these guidelines:

  • Ensure good lighting - natural daylight works best
  • Focus directly on the affected area of the plant
  • Include enough of the plant to show context (leaf, stem, etc.)
  • Avoid shadows or glare on the plant surface
  • Keep the camera steady to avoid blur
  • If possible, include both affected and healthy parts for comparison

What file types are supported for uploads?

Pestector supports common image formats including JPEG, PNG, and GIF. The maximum file size is 10MB. For best results, we recommend using high-resolution images but be aware that very large files may take longer to process.

What does the confidence score mean?

The confidence score represents how certain our AI model is about the diagnosis. A higher percentage indicates greater certainty. We categorize confidence as:

  • High confidence ( > 85%): The model is very certain of its diagnosis
  • Medium confidence (70-85%): The model is reasonably confident but some uncertainty exists
  • Low confidence (> 70%) : The model is less certain and expert review is recommended

Treatment Information

How reliable are the treatment recommendations?

Our treatment recommendations are based on agricultural best practices and are reviewed by experts. However, they should be considered as general guidance rather than definitive solutions. Local conditions, regulations, and the specific severity of the disease may require adaptations to our recommendations. For severe infestations or when uncertain, we recommend consulting with a local agricultural extension office or plant pathologist.

Does Pestector recommend organic solutions?

Yes, whenever possible, we provide both conventional and organic treatment options. Our treatment recommendations typically include cultural practices, biological controls, and organic remedies alongside conventional chemical treatments when necessary. Users can choose the approach that best aligns with their gardening or farming philosophy.

What if the recommended treatment doesn't work?

If the recommended treatments aren't effective, several factors could be at play:

  • The disease might be misidentified - try uploading a clearer image
  • The disease might be at an advanced stage making it harder to treat
  • There might be multiple issues affecting the plant simultaneously
  • Local environmental factors may be impacting effectiveness

You can contact us for additional support or consult with a local plant expert. We're continuously working to improve our recommendations based on user feedback.

Technical Questions

How does the AI technology work?

Pestector uses a deep learning neural network based on the VGG16 architecture, fine-tuned specifically for plant disease classification. The model has been trained on over 54,000 images from the PlantVillage dataset and other sources. When you upload an image, our system:

  1. Preprocesses the image for analysis
  2. Runs it through our neural network model
  3. Identifies the most likely diseases based on visual patterns
  4. Calculates confidence scores for each potential diagnosis
  5. Retrieves the appropriate treatment information

For more detailed information about our technology, visit our Research page.

Is there an API available for developers?

Yes, we offer a RESTful API for developers who want to integrate Pestector's plant disease classification capabilities into their own applications. Our API supports classification endpoints, category listings, and detailed disease information. API access is available with our Professional and Enterprise plans. For API documentation and access, please contact our support team or visit the API documentation section in your account dashboard.

What browsers and devices are supported?

Pestector is compatible with all modern browsers including:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

The platform is fully responsive and works on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. For the best mobile experience, we recommend using our mobile app available for iOS and Android devices.

How do I upgrade or change my subscription?

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard:

  1. Log in to your Pestector account
  2. Navigate to "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Subscription"
  4. Choose "Change Plan" or "Cancel Subscription"

Plan changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately and gain access to new features right away.
