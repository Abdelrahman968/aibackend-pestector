Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the plant disease detection?

Our system achieves 98.6% accuracy when tested on standard datasets. However, real-world conditions may affect accuracy, which is why we provide confidence scores with each analysis.

What file formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, GIF, and TIFF. For best results, use high-quality images with good lighting conditions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All uploads are encrypted and we don't share your data with third parties without your consent. Images are stored temporarily and can be deleted upon request.

Do you offer API access?

Yes, we provide API access for developers and businesses who want to integrate our plant disease detection capabilities into their applications. Contact us for details.
