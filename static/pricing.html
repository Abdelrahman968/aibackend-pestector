Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How accurate is the plant disease detection?

Our VGG16-based model has been fine-tuned to achieve 98.6% accuracy on the PlantVillage dataset. However, for rare diseases or unusual presentations, we recommend consulting with an agricultural expert.

What plants do you support?

We currently support 14 plant species and can identify 38 different plant-disease combinations. These include tomato, potato, apple, corn, grape, and more. We're constantly expanding our database.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service. Simply contact our support team.

How does the API work?

Our API allows you to integrate our plant disease detection capabilities into your own applications. It accepts image uploads and returns detailed analysis in JSON format. Full documentation is available for Enterprise customers.
